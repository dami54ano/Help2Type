Keyboard

Feel what you write

help2type is the mobile smartphone keyboard for visually impaired or blind people: help2type is as large as the keyboard on a larger smartphone (like the iPhone X), but haptically tangible. It is light, small and always with you when needed. This makes it a reliable companion not only for people with sensory impairments, but also for people with poor vision and older people.

The Benefits:

Tangible: Feel what you write
Mobile: Handy and easy to store
Fast: Instantly connected to smartphone
Secure: Can be magnetically attached to smartphone
Versatile: Can be attached to any smartphone and is optimized for 7 languages (English US, Spanish Spain, French France, German Germany, German Switzerland, Czech and Italian)
Communicative: Share and belong
Hygienic: Can be operated with thin protective gloves

Technical Specifications:

Layout: Printed qwertz, software adapted to selected language
Connection: BlueTooth 4.0
Weight: 40 grams
Dimensions/Size: Width 4.6 cm; Length 6.7 cm; Height 0.8 cm (Closed 1.4 cm)
Battery: 190 mAh (Lasts at least 6 weeks with frequent use)
Material: Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene Copolymer (ABS)
Waterproof: No
Package Contents: 1 Keyboard, 1 USB-C charging cable, 1 QR code to scan (for user manual)
