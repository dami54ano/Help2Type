Set Up the Keyboard

In this tutorial, you will learn how to set up the keyboard correctly. The basis is the familiar QWERTZ layout. For the symbols and special characters to be correct, you must set the hardware and software keyboard to Swiss German. Select Settings, General, Keyboard, Keyboards and select German Switzerland or French Switzerland QWERTZ. Now open Notes, for example, and tap "next keyboard" at the bottom left. Select German Switzerland.

Usage Instructions

How to write?

The keyboard is optimized for writing with both thumbs. The writing style was copied from today's youth, who do it intuitively this way. The size of your fingers doesn't matter with this style. After about 30 learning sessions, your brain automatically learns where the keys are. Other writing styles are possible.

Instructions:

Hold both palms facing upward. Bring your hands together until the pinky fingers touch on the sides. Place the smartphone with the screen facing up in your hand. The charging port must be aligned toward the palms. Place your thumbs on the keyboard. You can start typing right away.
